Avoid Recent Directors/Writers
Off by default. Directors & writers are always shown in My Dramas and the Crew Directory. Turning this ON also makes Check Cast and Recommendations flag/avoid dramas whose director or writer is in your last 10 - not just actors. Smart Score is unaffected (it already rewards familiar directors/writers on purpose).
